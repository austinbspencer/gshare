---

You have the ability to turn on automated reminders at the gallery level.

:::note[SMTP Required]
You must have your SMTP settings configured properly for this to work.
:::

## Reminder Email

The reminder will be sent out as an email to any email you want when the gallery is **5 days** from expiration. If you have the reminder email enabled, you can expect the email to be sent off at 8AM local time.

:::info Example Reminder Email

<div>
  <p>Hello,</p>
  <p>
    Just a quick heads-up: the gallery of images I shared with you will expire
    soon.
  </p>
  <p>
    <strong>Expiration Date:</strong> (Expiration Date)
  </p>
  <p>
    Please take a moment to review
    <a href="#">your gallery</a> before they're no longer accessible.
    If you have any questions or need an extension, feel free to reach out.
  </p>
  <p>Thanks!</p>

  <p>
    Best,<br />
    (Photographer Name)
  </p>
</div>
:::

> You can expect the `(Expiration Date)` and the `(Photographer Name)` to be replaced with their respective values when the reminder is sent.
